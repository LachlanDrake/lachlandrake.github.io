## About Me

I am a Ph.D. student at the University of Newcastle, Australia.

## Research Interests

- **Wireless Communication:** 6G networks, non-terrestrial networks (NTN), convex optimsation, resource allocation, signal processing, beamforming, channel models.
- **Satellite Communications:** low earth orbit (LEO) networks, direct-to-device (D2D), user scheduling, secure satellite communications.
- **Earth Observation:** synthetic aperture radar (SAR), wildfire detection, forest and vegetation monitoring, multi-sensor data fusion.
- **Deep Learning:** convolutional neural networks (CNN), transformer-based models, multi-modal learning.
- **Federated Learning:** federated optimization algorithms, communication-efficient federated learning, personalized federated learning.
